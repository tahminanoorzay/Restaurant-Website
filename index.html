Pizza
Pizza is one of the most popular foods in the world. It is originally from Italy. Pizza is made with dough, cheese, tomato sauce, and different toppings like chicken, vegetables, or meat. Many people enjoy pizza because it is delicious and easy to share with friends and family.
Burger
A burger is a famous fast food meal. It usually contains a bread bun, meat or chicken patty, cheese, lettuce, tomato, and sauce. Burgers are popular because they are quick to eat and come in many different flavors. People often eat burgers with fries and cold drinks.
Pasta
Pasta is another traditional food from Italy. It is made from wheat flour and water and comes in many shapes such as spaghetti and macaroni. Pasta is usually served with sauces, cheese, vegetables, or meat. It is loved for its soft texture and rich taste.
